ABOUT ME

Marina Lambrini Diamandis (born 10 October 1985), known mononymously as Marina (stylised as MARINA) and previously by the stage name Marina and the Diamonds, is a Welsh singer-songwriter. Born in Brynmawr and raised in Abergavenny, she moved to London as a teenager to become a professional singer, despite having little formal musical experience.
